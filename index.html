Biography

I’m a PhD student at Nara Institute of Science and Technologhy (NAIST) engaged in Social Computing Laboratory, and a Project Researcher at Institute of Scientific and Industrial Research Osaka University (Sanken) engaged in Sakurai・Matsubara Laboratory. I am interested in tackling some problems related to computer social science (mainly fake news and political polarization) and time series problems by leveraging naturla language processing and time series modeling.
